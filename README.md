# ZoeTrack 

> Your personal spiritual growth companion that automatically tracks your prayer life and helps you stay consistent in your walk with God.

---

## What It Does

ZoeTrack is a spiritual growth tracker that helps Christians maintain consistent devotional habits without the stress of manual tracking. 

You simply tell ZoeTrack your spiritual goals for the week or month in plain English (like talking to a friend), and it automatically creates prayer trackers, organizes your sermon notes, reminds you to listen to messages, and even **detects when you're praying** to track your personal time with God. No more forgotten prayer times or scattered spiritual notes.

---

## Why It Matters

**The Problem:**

- You miss prayer times because life gets busy and you forget to track them manually
- Your sermon notes, revelations, and spiritual insights are scattered across different apps
- Setting up habit trackers feels like extra work instead of helping your spiritual life
- Most trackers are pass/fail systems that feel legalistic instead of grace-focused

**How We Fix It:**

ZoeTrack removes the friction from spiritual discipline. It automatically detects when you're praying (no manual start button!), intelligently organizes your notes by year/month/category, and uses a grace-focused color system (not just pass/fail) to show your progress. You spend less time tracking and more time actually growing.

---

## Who It's For

- **Busy Christians**: People who want to grow spiritually but struggle with consistency and tracking
- **Church members**: Believers who take sermon notes and want them organized by date and category
- **Prayer warriors**: Anyone serious about spending quality time with God and wants accountability
- **Ministry leaders**: Pastors and leaders who want to track their spiritual disciplines and message consumption

## Key Features

### 1. Natural Language Goal Setting
Write your spiritual goals conversationally at the start of each week/month. Example: "I want to pray 30 minutes daily for my family, listen to one message per week by Pastor X, and track my joy expressions for 5 minutes daily." ZoeTrack parses this and creates all the trackers automatically.

### 2. Automatic Prayer Detection
The game-changer: ZoeTrack detects when you're praying during the day using context signals (phone stationary, time patterns). It prompts "It sounds like you are praying, should we start tracking it?" and automatically times your prayer session. No manual toggles needed.
